SBLT Whitepaper

1. Introduction

SBLT (SHIB BONE LEASH TREAT) is a composite token built on the Shibarium network, designed to unify the core tokens of the Shiba Inu ecosystem—BONE, SHIB, LEASH, and TREAT—into a single, versatile asset. By leveraging Shibarium's high-throughput Layer 2 blockchain, SBLT offers a seamless mechanism for users to mint and burn tokens, enhancing liquidity and interoperability within the ecosystem.

This whitepaper outlines the vision, mechanics, technical implementation, and potential use cases of SBLT, providing a comprehensive guide for users, developers, and stakeholders.

2. Token Overview

SBLT is an ERC20-compatible token that represents a fixed combination of BONE (Shibarium's native gas token), SHIB, LEASH, and TREAT. Users can mint SBLT by depositing a predefined set of these tokens and burn SBLT to redeem the underlying assets, ensuring a transparent and trustless process.

3. Token Mechanics

SBLT operates on a straightforward mint-and-burn mechanism, allowing users to:

  • Mint SBLT: Deposit 1 set of tokens (1 BONE, 20,000 SHIB, 0.0005 LEASH, 50 TREAT) to receive 10,000,000 SBLT.
  • Burn SBLT: Redeem 10,000,000 SBLT to retrieve 1 set of tokens (1 BONE, 20,000 SHIB, 0.0005 LEASH, 50 TREAT).

The exchange ratio is fixed, ensuring predictability and fairness. BONE, as Shibarium's native gas token, is deposited via `msg.value`, while SHIB, LEASH, and TREAT are transferred as ERC20 tokens.

4. Technical Implementation

The SBLT smart contract is deployed on Shibarium, leveraging Solidity for robust and secure functionality. Key features include:

  • ERC20 Compliance: Supports standard functions (`transfer`, `approve`, `transferFrom`) for seamless integration with wallets and dApps.
  • Deposit Function: Accepts BONE (payable) and ERC20 tokens, mints SBLT, and enforces balance and allowance checks.
  • Withdraw Function: Burns SBLT and returns BONE and ERC20 tokens, with strict balance validation.

5. Tokenomics

SBLT's tokenomics are designed to align with the Shiba Inu ecosystem and ensure long-term sustainability:

  • Total Supply: Capped at 1,000,000,000,000,000 SBLT, minted only through deposits.
  • Mint/Burn Ratio: Fixed at 1 set (1 BONE, 20,000 SHIB, 0.0005 LEASH, 50 TREAT) = 10,000,000 SBLT.
  • No Fees: The contract does not impose additional fees beyond Shibarium gas costs (paid in BONE).
  • Transparency: All minting and burning activities are recorded on-chain, verifiable via Shibarium Scan.

The fixed ratio ensures that SBLT maintains a stable relationship with its underlying tokens, reducing volatility and enhancing trust.
